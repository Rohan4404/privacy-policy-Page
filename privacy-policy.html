Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

Welcome to PowerSense. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information during the onboarding and usage of our app:

  • Organization Name
  • GST Number
  • Organization Address
  • Organization Type (e.g., Manufacturing, Hardware, Software)
  • Admin Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Machine Data (Current, Power, Units, etc.) via hardware sensors and APIs

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information to:

  • Register and verify organizations and admins
  • Onboard clients through the registered email for secure access
  • Display machine data in graphical format to help users monitor usage
  • Allow Super Admin to view data from all onboarded admins and machines

3. Information Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. Only verified Admins and Super Admins have access to the machine data within the organization.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal and machine data from unauthorized access.

5. Your Choices

You may request the deletion of your account or data by contacting us via email.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
